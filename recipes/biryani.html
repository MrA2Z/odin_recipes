Biryani

Rice and Chicken dish with spicy ingredients

Biryani known for the aroma and flavour. It is rice dish with chcicken or mutton and spices.

The dish is popular in Pakistan and neighbouring countries.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Herbs

Steps:

  1. Boil the rice
  2. Prepare the chicken
  3. Prepare the herbs and mix with chicken
  4. Mix the rice and chicken in layers
  5. Put them on steam
  6. The dish should be ready in couple of minutes
