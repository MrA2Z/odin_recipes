Falooda

Cold dessert made with ice cream and noodles

Falooda is a popular cuisine in Pakistan. Tradionally it is made with mixing ice crea, vermicalli, basil seeds and milk

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Ice cream
  • Basil seeds
  • vermicalli
  • Rooh Afza

Steps:

  1. Boil the milk
  2. Mix Lazziza falooda
  3. Cook it for few minutes
  4. Garnish with pistachio and jelly
  5. Put in the fridge
  6. Serve it cold after adding ice cream
